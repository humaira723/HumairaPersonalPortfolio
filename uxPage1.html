Contractor Company Site Design

The wireframe

This is the initial wireframe for a Contractor Company. I wanted the site to display a slideshow at the top of the page behind the company name. Under the About Us section, there is a services section displaying nine services offered by the company. There are three featured projects section with a link to a button that redirects the user to a different page which will display all their projects for the user to view.

The Final Design

I chose a minimal color pallette to highlight the important details regarding the company. For example, I used a bright red to bring attention to the many services they offer. I added a divider image under the services section to create a transition to the projects section of the site which contains more information. In the Projects page, I decided to display the projects in different positions so the page can look balanced.
